<p><span style="font-size:18px">Respawn Delay is a mod designed for servers. It fits best in a pvp server, but can be used in events as well. Instead of instantly respawning, players enter spectator mode on death. They can fly around and see the action, until the configurable delay has been reached and they respawn. All messages sent are configurable, but by default the spectating player will receive an update on when they will respawn every second counting down. There is also a <em>/respawnall</em> command to force all spectating players to respawn.<br><br><em>Respawn delay is compatible with Gravestone mods. Just make sure to disable dropItemsOnDeath in the config to prevent duplication.</em></span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/server.svg" alt="Environment: Server" width="220" height="49"></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/configurable.svg" alt="Configurable" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://serilum.com/configuration-guide" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/configuration-guide.svg" alt="Configuration guide" width="220" height="49"></a></p><p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span style="font-size:18px">All available config options:</span></p><div class="spoiler" style="margin-left:10px;padding-left:10px"><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>ignoreAdministratorPlayers</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default false</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If enabled, player operators/administrators will not be put in spectator mode on death.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>ignoreCreativePlayers</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If enabled, player in creative mode will not be put in spectator mode on death.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>keepItemsOnDeath</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default false</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If enabled, players will keep their items on death, and no drop event will be ran. This will also ignore the 'dropItemsOnDeath' config.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>dropItemsOnDeath</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If enabled, players will drop their items on death as normal vanilla behaviour.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>respawnAtWorldSpawn</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If enabled, players will be respawned at the world spawn point. If disabled, they'll respawn wherever they're spectating.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>respawnWhenPlayerLogsOut</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If enabled, players will respawn when they logout while waiting for a respawn. Prevents players from getting stuck in spectator mode.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>respawnWhenPlayerLogsIn</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If enabled, players will respawn when they log in and are still in spectator mode. Prevents players from getting stuck in spectator mode.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>respawnDelayInSeconds</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 10, min -1, max 3600</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">The delay in seconds after which a spectating player will respawn. A value of -1 makes players never respawn automatically. The '/respawnall' command can still be used.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>onDeathMessage</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default "You died! Your gamemode has been set to spectator."</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">The message which is sent to the player on death. Leave empty to disable.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>onRespawnMessage</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default "You respawned."</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">The message which is sent to players when they respawn. Leave empty to disable.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>waitingForRespawnMessage</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default "You will respawn in &lt;seconds_left&gt; seconds."</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">The message which is sent to players when they are waiting to be respawned. The text '&lt;seconds_left&gt;' will be replaced with the actual seconds left. Leave empty to disable.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>lowestPossibleYCoordinate</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default -10, min -50, max 256</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">When a player falls into the void, this determines the y position that's set after when a player enters spectator mode.</span></p></div><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/commands.svg" alt="Commands" width="838" height="64"></p><p><span style="font-size:10px"><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>/respawnall</strong> - Respawns all spectating players.
