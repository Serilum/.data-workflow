<p><span style="font-size:18px">Ice Prevents Crop Growth is a mod made to make new design features possible without adding blocks. For example a half-grown green melon stem next to your desk, or a short cactus without string above it. When an ice block is placed underneath any block with a crop, the crop will never mature to the next growth stage. This works for both vanilla and modded crops. You can still use bone meal to get to the desired stage.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/the-vanilla-experience" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/the-vanilla-experience.svg" alt="Included in The Vanilla Experience" width="350" height="49"></a><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/serilums-rpg-bundle" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/rpg-bundle.svg" alt="Included in Serilum's RPG Bundle" width="334" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/server.svg" alt="Environment: Server" width="220" height="49"></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/showcase.svg" alt="Showcase" width="838" height="64"></p><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Works for every crop with growth stages:</strong></span><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/projects/ice-prevents-crop-growth/a.jpg" alt="" width="1142" height="614"></p>
