Right-clicking the anvil with an iron ingot will repair it one stage, saving you some iron to repair it before it breaks completely. This will consume the ingot.<br><br>Right-clicking it with obsidian will damage it one, until in its most damaged stage. It won't break the block. This will allow for extra design purposes without having to use lots of experience to damage it manually.
