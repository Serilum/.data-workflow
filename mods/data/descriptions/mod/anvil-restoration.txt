<p><span style="font-size:18px">Anvil Restoration adds the ability to repair and damage placed anvils in the world. Right-clicking the anvil with an iron ingot will repair it one stage, saving you some iron to repair it before it breaks completely. This will consume the ingot.<br><br>Right-clicking it with obsidian will damage it one, until in its most damaged stage. It won't break the block. This will allow for extra design purposes without having to use lots of experience to damage it manually. The obsidian will not be consumed.<br><br></span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/the-vanilla-experience" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/the-vanilla-experience.svg" alt="Included in The Vanilla Experience" width="350" height="49"></a><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/serilums-rpg-bundle" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/rpg-bundle.svg" alt="Included in Serilum's RPG Bundle" width="334" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/server.svg" alt="Environment: Server" width="220" height="49"></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/showcase.svg" alt="Showcase" width="838" height="64"></p><p>Repairing an anvil:</p><div class="spoiler"><p><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/projects/anvil-restoration/a.webp" alt="" width="1000" height="522"></p></div><p>&nbsp;<br><span style="font-size:14px"><span style="font-size:14px"><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Damaging an anvil:</strong></span></span><br></span></p><div class="spoiler"><p><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/projects/anvil-restoration/b.webp" alt=""></p></div>
