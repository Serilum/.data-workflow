<p><span style="font-size:18px">What Durability? removes all durability from items, allowing players to use them infinitely. This is similar to other games such as Terraria and Stardew Valley. You probably wouldn't use this in a general playthrough, but it can be used for RPG modpacks where the main goal isn't to gather resources, but to accomplish a certain objective.
