It's fully configurable. Harvest crop blocks (pumpkins/melons) faster, be able to un-till soil and increased till range depending on the tier (e.g. wood/diamond). By default you'll have to crouch for the increased range.<br><br>The hoe used with increased range will be damaged for each extra block tilled.
