<p><span style="font-size:18px">Humbling Bundle is a minimalistic mod that doubles all entity loot, in order to speed up early game progress or make late-game grinds less cumbersome. Inspired by the identically titled item in the binding of Isaac.</span><br><br></p><p><strong>Simply Explained</strong><br>You kill a chicken, if the loot without the mod would have been 1 feather and 2 chicken, now it's 2 feathers and 4 chicken.</p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/server.svg" alt="Environment: Server" width="220" height="49"></p>
