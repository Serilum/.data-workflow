<p><span style="font-size:18px">Enchantment Level Cap Indicator is a client-side mod that helps players determine whether an enchantment on an item is maxed out. While more experienced players have each level cap memorized, new adventurers do not. A symbol is placed next to the enchantment level when it has reached its maximum potential. By default a golden star is used, though you can change this to whatever you prefer in the config.<br><br>Works in the enchantment table, anvil and inventory item tool tips.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/the-vanilla-experience" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/the-vanilla-experience.svg" alt="Included in The Vanilla Experience" width="350" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/client.svg" alt="Environment: Client" width="220" height="49"></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/configurable.svg" alt="Configurable" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://serilum.com/configuration-guide" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/configuration-guide.svg" alt="Configuration guide" width="220" height="49"></a></p><p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span style="font-size:18px">All available config options:</span></p><div class="spoiler" style="margin-left:10px;padding-left:10px"><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>maxLevelSymbol</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default "⭐"</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">The symbol used to show that an enchantment is the maximum level.
