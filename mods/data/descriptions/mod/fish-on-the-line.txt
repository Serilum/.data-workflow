<p><span style="font-size:18px">Fish On The Line is a mod which can help you catch fish without your full attention. When you hold a bell in your offhand while fishing, it will make a sound whenever there's a fish on the line. You could watch something else and just right-click when you hear a sound. <br><br>Works well with the&nbsp;<a style="font-size:18px" href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/recast"
