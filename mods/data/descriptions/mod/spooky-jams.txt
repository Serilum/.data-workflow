While the naming is deliberately intended as a pun, it adds actual "spooky" jams which when consumed give the player various potion effects. It also adds the Spooky Yam crop. Which can be turned into a Spooky Yam Jam!<br><br>All jams can be shapelessly crafted. The base ingredients are a glass jar and sugar. Each jam has two unique ingredients that make up its colour and effects.
