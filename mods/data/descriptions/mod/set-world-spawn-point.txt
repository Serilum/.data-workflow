<p><span style="font-size:18px">Set World Spawn is a minimalistic configurable mod which allows setting an exact custom spawn point for newly created worlds. It can also force players to spawn at the exact world spawn, instead of around it, which happens in the default vanilla behaviour.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img
