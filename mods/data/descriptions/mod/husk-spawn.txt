<p><span style="font-size:18px">Husk Spawn is a minimalistic configurable mod which adds a chance for husks to spawn naturally everywhere instead of a zombie spawn. By default they only spawn in a desert biome. Husks do not burn in daylight as they have some sort of hat on.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/the-vanilla-experience" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/the-vanilla-experience.svg" alt="Included in The Vanilla Experience" width="350" height="49"></a><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/serilums-spawn-bundle" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/spawn-bundle.svg" alt="Included in Serilum's Spawn Bundle" width="350" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/server.svg" alt="Environment: Server" width="220" height="49"></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/configurable.svg" alt="Configurable" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://serilum.com/configuration-guide" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/configuration-guide.svg" alt="Configuration guide" width="220" height="49"></a></p><p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span style="font-size:18px">All available config options:</span></p><div class="spoiler" style="margin-left:10px;padding-left:10px"><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>chanceZombieIsHusk</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 0.15, min 0, max 1.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">&nbsp;The chance a zombie that has spawned is of the husk variant.</span></p></div>
