Shulkers can quickly become difficult to find in a world, because of the usefulness of shulker boxes. Normally you'd have to find new shulker spawns after your first farming trip, or create an intricate farm to duplicate shulkers with shulker bullets. With this mod, you'll be able to stay in the same place and farm shulker shells infinitely.&nbsp;Shulkers can still teleport, leaving it balanced.<br><br>By default a shulker will respawn in its death location after 60 seconds, although this can be changed in the config. When a server (or singleplayer world) shuts down, all pending shulker spawns will process immediately.<br><br>Shulkers that originate from a mob spawner by default won't respawn.</span></p><h2><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></h2><p><a
