<p><span style="font-size:18px">Full Brightness Toggle is a client-side mod which allows players to instantly turn the brightness (gamma) to full with the press of a button. By default it's G, but this can be changed in Options -&gt; Controls. Created after reading a request from someone with impaired eyesight, who needed this feature to play the game. Don't use it on servers where it's not allowed.
