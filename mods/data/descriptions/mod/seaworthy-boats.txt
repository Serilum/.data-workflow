Boats have health and armour now, and can be repaired. Each tier improves the amount of armour, and the speed of the boat.<br><br>The speed and armour of each tier can be set to your preference via the config.</span></p><h2><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></h2><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/both.svg" alt="Environment: Client &amp; Server" width="293" height="65"></p><h2><br><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/configurable.svg" alt="Configurable" width="838" height="64"></h2><p><a href="https://serilum.com/configuration-guide" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/configuration-guide.svg" alt="Configuration guide" width="220" height="49"></a></p><p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span style="font-size:18px">All available config options:</span></p><div class="spoiler" style="margin-left:10px;padding-left:10px"><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>disablePlayerBoatDamage</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">Whether a player's own hits deal no damage to boats. When on, hitting a boat tells you to sneak and right-click empty-handed to pick it up instead. Mobs and other sources still damage boats.</span><br><br></p><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>showBoatHealthBar</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">Whether to show the boat's health as a row of hearts on the HUD while riding it, like a horse's health. Hides the hunger bar while in a boat, the same way vanilla mounts do.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>boatMaxHealth</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 20, min 1, max 1000</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">The amount of health a boat has before it breaks. Each point of incoming damage lowers it by one (a trident hit deals around 8). Vanilla boats break after about 4 damage.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>healthPerPlank</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 2, min 1, max 100</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How much boat health a single plank restores when repairing at a shipyard in repair mode.</span><br><br></p><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>shipyardRange</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 4, min 1, max 16</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How close a boat must be to a shipyard block for it to be repaired or upgraded there, in blocks.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>shipyardInfoYOffset</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 8, min 0, max 1000</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How far down from the top of the screen, in pixels, the shipyard info panel is drawn when you look at a shipyard. Increase it to move the panel below other mods' block tooltips, such as Jade or WAILA.</span><br><br></p><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>sequentialUpgrades</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">Whether a boat must be reinforced one tier at a time, in order: copper, gold, iron, diamond, netherite. When off, a boat can be upgraded straight to any higher tier with the matching material.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>materialsPerUpgrade</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 8, min 1, max 64</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How many of a material it costs to reinforce a boat to its tier. Reinforce a boat at a shipyard set to upgrade mode. Netherite reinforcement costs half this amount.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>copperArmour</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 0.07, min 0, max 1.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How much incoming damage a copper-reinforced boat ignores. 0.0 is no protection, 1.0 is full immunity.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>goldArmour</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 0.10, min 0, max 1.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How much incoming damage a gold-reinforced boat ignores. 0.0 is no protection, 1.0 is full immunity.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>ironArmour</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 0.25, min 0, max 1.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How much incoming damage an iron-reinforced boat ignores. 0.0 is no protection, 1.0 is full immunity.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>diamondArmour</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 0.40, min 0, max 1.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How much incoming damage a diamond-reinforced boat ignores. 0.0 is no protection, 1.0 is full immunity.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>netheriteArmour</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 0.80, min 0, max 1.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How much incoming damage a netherite-reinforced boat ignores. 0.0 is no protection, 1.0 is full immunity.</span><br><br></p><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>copperSpeed</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 1.10, min 0.1, max 20.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">Forward speed multiplier for a copper-reinforced boat. 1.0 is vanilla boat speed, 1.5 is fifty percent faster.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>goldSpeed</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 1.20, min 0.1, max 20.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">Forward speed multiplier for a gold-reinforced boat. 1.0 is vanilla boat speed, 1.5 is fifty percent faster.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>ironSpeed</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 1.35, min 0.1, max 20.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">Forward speed multiplier for an iron-reinforced boat. 1.0 is vanilla boat speed, 1.5 is fifty percent faster.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>diamondSpeed</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 1.55, min 0.1, max 20.0</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">Forward speed multiplier for a diamond-reinforced boat. 1.0 is vanilla boat speed, 1.5 is fifty percent faster.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>netheriteSpeed</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 1.80, min 0.1, max
