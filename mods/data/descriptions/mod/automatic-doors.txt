<p><span style="font-size:18px">Automatic Doors is a minimalistic configurable mod which automatically opens doors around the player and closes them with a delay. The doors will only be closed if the player isn't near after opening. By default iron doors will also be opened, but you may change that in the config.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img
