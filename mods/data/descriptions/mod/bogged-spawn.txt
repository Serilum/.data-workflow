<p><span style="font-size:18px">Bogged Spawn adds a chance for the bogged entity to spawn naturally everywhere in the world in place of a skeleton. By default they only spawn in swamps and mangrove swamps but now you can encounter them everywhere! Adds a bigger challenge to your world, since their poison arrows can be daunting. There is a 1/10 chance that a spawned skeleton is of the bogged variant, which can be altered in the config.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img
