<p><span style="font-size:18px">Replanting Crops is a minimalistic mod which automatically replants crops harvested with seeds from drops. By default a hoe must be held for the replanting to occur, but this can be toggled in the config. By holding the sneak button, you can prevent the replanting as well.<br><br>Version 4.0 adds support for modded crops that extend the CropBlock.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img
