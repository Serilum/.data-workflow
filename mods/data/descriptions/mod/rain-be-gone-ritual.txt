<p><span style="font-size:18px">Rain Be Gone Ritual is a mod which was created to give players in survival a (somewhat) logical way to stop the rain. All you need is some cauldrons, a log and a flint &amp; steel. Place the cauldrons (minimum 4, maximum 8) around the log and set the log on fire. After the ritual there will be a harmless explosion effect, the cauldrons will be filled with water and the rain will stop.&nbsp;See the gif below for the effect.</span></p><p
