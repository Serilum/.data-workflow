<p><span style="font-size:18px">Bigger Sponge Absorption Radius extends the functionality of the sponge block. By placing multiple blocks next to each other, the absorption radius increases. Whenever the water hits a dry (non-wet) sponge, it absorps water in the combined radius around that block. Uses a recursive function to count how many sponge blocks are placed next to each other, whenever they are neighbouring blocks. The radius is calculated by the default sponge radius * neighbouring sponge block count. Wet sponges are counted but cannot start the absorption process.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/the-vanilla-experience" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/the-vanilla-experience.svg" alt="Included in The Vanilla Experience" width="350" height="49"></a><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/serilums-extra-bundle" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/extra-bundle.svg" alt="Included in Serilum's Extra Bundle" width="350" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/server.svg" alt="Environment: Server" width="220" height="49"></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/showcase.svg" alt="Showcase" width="838" height="64"></p><p><span style="font-size:14px"><em>All sponge blocks used will be turned into a wet sponge.</em></span></p><div class="spoiler"><p><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/projects/bigger-sponge-absorption-radius/a.webp" alt=""></p></div>
