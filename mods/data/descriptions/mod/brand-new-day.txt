It's a nice transition between night and day. The header image at the top can be changed inside the config folder.<br><br>The statistics it currently shows are: Blocks broken, blocks travelled, deaths, items crafted and monsters slain.<br></span></p><h2><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></h2><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/client.svg" alt="Environment: Client" width="220" height="49"></p><h2><br><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/configurable.svg" alt="Configurable" width="838" height="64"></h2><p><a href="https://serilum.com/configuration-guide" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/configuration-guide.svg" alt="Configuration guide" width="220" height="49"></a></p><p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span style="font-size:18px">All available config options:</span></p><div class="spoiler" style="margin-left:10px;padding-left:10px"><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>showHeader</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If the header image should be shown at the top of the new day screen.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>showDayCount</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If the current day number should be shown on the new day screen.</span><br><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>showStats</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If a summary of the previous day should be shown. true</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">If a sound plays when the new day screen appears.</span><br><br></p><p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>newDayScreenDurationTicks</strong></span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#8a8d91">&nbsp;&nbsp;default 200, min 1, max 1200</span><br><span style="font-size:13px">How many ticks the new day screen stays on screen in total. 20 ticks = 1
