<p><span style="font-size:18px">Placeable Blaze Rods is a minimalistic mod which gives a custom block texture to blaze rods. Similar to end rods, they can now be placed anywhere in the world and emit light. They can of course still be transformed into blaze powder and keep their normal use.</span></p><p><br><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>The design is based on the end rod, with the blaze rod's colour pallet.
