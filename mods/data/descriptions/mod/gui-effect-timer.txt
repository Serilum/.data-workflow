<p><span style="font-size:18px">GUI Effect Timer is a client-side mod that adds timers to the bottom of effect badges on the screen.&nbsp;It works with abbreviations for seconds, minutes, hours and days. Updated every tick, it allows you to see the exact moment your effects run out. Very handy for when you're in lava with fire resistance!<br><br>The text colour is easily changed within the config. White by default.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a
