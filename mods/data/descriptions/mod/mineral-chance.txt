<p><span style="font-size:18px">Mineral Chance is a mod created to make mining a little bit more exciting. It adds a chance to find a random mineral after mining a stone-type block. In the overworld these stone blocks are <strong><em>stone, andesite, diorite, granite, tuff and deepslate</em></strong>. In the nether it's <em><strong>netherrack, basalt and blackstone</strong></em>.<br><br>By default there is a 1/50 chance to find a mineral. The overworld minerals consist of <em><strong>diamonds, iron nuggets, gold nuggets, lapis lazuli, redstone and emeralds</strong></em>. The nether minerals consist of <em><strong>quartz, golden nuggets and netherite scraps(1.16+)</strong></em>. Mining with Silk Touch will disable mineral drops.<br><br>All minerals have the same chance of dropping. The message when finding a random mineral can be changed and turned off in the config.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img
