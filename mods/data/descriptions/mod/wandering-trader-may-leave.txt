<p><span style="font-size:18px">Wandering Trader May Leave adds a simple way to ask the wandering trader to exit the map without resorting to violence. It adds a small button in the top left of the trade screen. When clicked, the trader disappears in a cloud of particles. You no longer have to kill it to get some peace and quiet.<br><br>The Trader Llamas disappear as well, but only if they are leashed to the trader. This behaviour can be changed in the config.</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><br><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/dependencies.svg" alt="Dependencies" width="838" height="64"></p><p><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/collective" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/requires/collective.svg" alt="Requires Collective" width="251" height="49"></a><br><a href="https://curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/the-vanilla-experience" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/dependencies/includes/the-vanilla-experience.svg" alt="Included in The Vanilla Experience" width="350" height="49"></a><br><img src="https://cdn.serilum.com/description/badges/environment/both.svg" alt="Environment: Client &amp;
